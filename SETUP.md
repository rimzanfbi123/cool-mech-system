*Goal: Ensuring you never lose your data.*

**Frequency (Mokakda karanna ona?):**
*   **Normal:** Sathiye sarayak (Weekly).
*   **Busy:** Davasa avasane (Daily at night).
*   **Crucial:** Phone eka maru karanna kalin hari Chrome cache clear karanna kalin aniwa karanna.

**Correct Way (Harima Vidiya):**
1.  Go to **Settings** in the app.
2.  Click **"Backup Now (Download File)"**.
3.  A file like `CMS_Backup_2026-02-12.json` will download.
4.  **DO NOT KEEP IT ONLY ON THE PHONE.**
5.  Immediately **Share/Send** that file to:
    *   Your own **Email**.
    *   Your own **WhatsApp**.
    *   Upload it to your **Google Drive** folder manually.
